Cartoon Bumper

Front-end Back-end Design Animation Video Editing
View

What problem you solved, how you approached it, and the impact.

Overview

Short animated bumper designed to introduce a fictional cartoon channel. The goal was to capture a playful tone while keeping timings tight and legible for broadcast.

Programs used

  • Adobe After Effects
  • Adobe Illustrator
  • Adobe Premiere Pro

Problems faced and solved

The main challenge was keeping the animation energetic without overwhelming the viewer. I solved this by simplifying shapes, limiting the color palette, and refining easing curves so the motion feels intentional instead of chaotic.

Custom Earbuds

Front-end Back-end Design Animation Video Editing
View

What problem you solved, how you approached it, and the impact.

Overview

Interactive product concept for custom earbuds. The focus was on presenting the model in a way that feels tactile and premium while still loading quickly in the browser.

Programs used

  • Blender
  • Substance Painter
  • Figma
  • Visual Studio Code

Problems faced and solved

The model was initially too heavy for the web. I reduced polygons, optimized textures, and adjusted lighting to keep the file size manageable while still showing enough detail for the hotspots to feel meaningful.
